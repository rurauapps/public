Privacy Policy – Padavallika

Last updated: 26 January 2026

Padavallika (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Padavallika – Telugu Words mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

Padavallika does not require users to provide personal information to browse, read, or learn Telugu words.

Authentication Information (Admin Access Only)

For administrative purposes, Padavallika may use Firebase Authentication. This may include an email address used only to identify admin users.

Usage & Technical Data

Padavallika does not collect personally identifiable usage data. Limited anonymous technical data (such as crash reports) may be collected to improve app stability.

Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. Strict security rules are applied, and write access is restricted to authorized admins.

Data Sharing

Padavallika does not sell, share, or misuse user data. No personal data is shared with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Padavallika is suitable for users of all ages. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Third-Party Services

Padavallika uses Google Firebase for backend services. Firebase may collect limited technical data in accordance with Google’s privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
